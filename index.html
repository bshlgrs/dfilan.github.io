About me

I'm a student of the Australian National University, enrolled in the honours program in computer science, studying the mathematics of AI. I am also an effective altruist, which means that I try to make as many charitable donations that improve the world as much as possible, and a vegan, because I believe that non-human animals matter morally. I also run the Less Wrong Canberra meetup, and am a proud member of People for the Ethical Treatment of Reinforcement Learners. Feel free to read some recipes for meals that I enjoy, have a look at my effective altruist profile, look at how I have made Vimperator work like Emacs at my Github repository, or contact me at df@danielfilan.com.
